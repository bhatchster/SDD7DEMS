Endorsement Process

[Placeholder — replace with the club's official endorsement process and timeline.]

1. Candidate Application

Candidates seeking the club's endorsement submit a questionnaire and request a screening interview.

2. Screening Interview

The endorsement committee meets with each candidate to discuss their record and priorities for District 7.

3. Committee Recommendation

The endorsement committee reviews all candidate materials and presents a recommendation to the membership.

4. Membership Vote

Eligible members vote at a general meeting. A candidate must reach the required threshold to be endorsed.

5. Endorsement Announcement

Endorsed candidates are announced publicly and supported through club outreach and volunteer efforts.
