About Our Club

The San Diego District 7 Democratic Club brings together residents, activists, and local leaders who are committed to electing Democrats up and down the ballot and advancing progressive values in our community. We host regular meetings, candidate forums, and volunteer events so neighbors can get involved in the issues that matter most to District 7.

Whether you're a longtime organizer or getting involved in local politics for the first time, there's a place for you in our club.

Our Platform

Affordable Housing

Supporting policies that increase housing supply and keep San Diego affordable for working families.

Public Safety & Justice

Advocating for community-centered public safety and a fair, accountable justice system.

Climate & Environment

Pushing for clean energy, climate resilience, and protection of San Diego's coastline and parks.

Education

Championing fully funded public schools and equitable access to education for every child.

Healthcare Access

Working toward affordable, accessible healthcare for all District 7 residents.

Economic Opportunity

Supporting small businesses, workers' rights, and pathways to good local jobs.
